About Styling AI

Styling AI uses AI to analyze your skin tone and suggest personalized fashion recommendations—from outfits and accessories to shopping links at Amazon.in, Myntra, and Zara.

Upload a photo, and our system detects your face, extracts RGB values, and classifies your skin tone (Fair, Medium, Olive, or Deep). You'll receive gender-specific outfit ideas, hairstyle tips, color palettes, and curated product links tailored to your unique look.
